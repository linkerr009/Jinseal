Description

Spiral wound gasket for industrial flange sealing.

Spiral wound gasket is commonly selected for pipelines, heat exchangers, pressure vessels, valves and other industrial flange connections. The gasket structure helps maintain sealing reliability when equipment faces temperature cycling, vibration or pressure fluctuation.

JinSeal can recommend suitable metal strip, filler material, ring type and gasket dimensions according to working medium, temperature, pressure, flange standard and drawing requirements.
